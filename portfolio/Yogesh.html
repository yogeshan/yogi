Process Flow

Wireframes
I sit down with blank papers and begin creating schematics and wireframing out the designs, giving careful thought to position, size and proportions. The next step is delving into Illustrator to finalise the wireframes to ensure a great user experience.
Design
Its Photoshop time! Taking the carefully crafted wireframes & userflows into consideration, I start to flesh out the user interface until Im happy with it. Then its the time for little tweaks until the client is as well.
Development
Heres the really fun part. Taking those designs and using my frontend skills to make them awesome. Whether it be a quick static site, a content-managed site, or even an e-commerce store - its going to work extremely well.
launch
Now all the hard work is done theres just a few final touches until the site can go live, and I can celebrate by sending over the final invoice. What a treat.
